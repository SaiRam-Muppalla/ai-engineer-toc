# Product Design Exercise: Your AI Product

Answer each question to define your AI product concept.

---

## 1. Problem Definition

**What problem are you solving?**
> _Write 1–2 sentences. Be specific. "Developers waste 30 minutes a day writing boilerplate code."_

**Who has this problem? (be specific)**
> _"Junior Python developers at startups, 1–5 years experience"_

**How painful is this problem? (1–10)**
> _Rate it. 8+ means people will pay._

**How do people solve it today?**
> _What's the current alternative? Google? Excel? Hiring someone?_

---

## 2. Solution

**What does your AI product do?**
> _One sentence: "My product does X for Y so they can Z."_

**Why does this need AI?** (not just a CRUD app)
> _AI adds value when it: generates content, understands language, finds patterns, makes predictions._

**What's your MVP? (minimum viable product)**
> _What's the smallest thing you can build in 2 weeks that delivers value?_

---

## 3. Monetization

**Who will pay for this?**
> _Consumers (B2C) or businesses (B2B)? B2B is usually easier to monetize._

**What's your pricing model?**
> _Freemium / Subscription / Usage-based / One-time_

**What's your pricing?**
> _Monthly price. Research competitors. Most AI tools: $10–$50/month._

**Estimated AI cost per user per month?**
> _Number of API calls × cost per call. Make sure margin is positive._

---

## 4. Validation

**How will you get your first 10 users?**
> _Post on Reddit? Share in Discord? Cold email? ProductHunt launch?_

**What question would you ask 10 potential users this week?**
> _"What's the hardest part of X for you?" NOT "Would you use this?"_

---

## 5. Competition

**Who are your competitors?**
> _List 3 competitors and what they charge._

**What's your unfair advantage?**
> _Why will users choose you? Cheaper? Faster? Better UX? Niche focus?_

---

## ✅ Validation Checklist

Before writing any code:

- [ ] Talked to 10 potential users
- [ ] Found 3 people who said "I'd pay $X for this"
- [ ] Researched 3 competitors
- [ ] Defined what success looks like in 3 months
- [ ] Estimated AI cost per user (is it profitable?)
